E's Journal..

... is one stop for parents to keep track of their child's appointments, activities and milestones Thought up by a mum who was tired of making countless notes on scraps of paper and calenders. This platform aims to provide a solution to all planning and organising needs related to your child. You can make notes regarding your child's health, track their progress at school and mark major milestones. All in one place! Learn more..

How it works...

It's as simple as ABC. Sign up! You can add information for all of your children using one account. You can give access to your partner as well so you both are in the loop. There are different tabs such as Health, School, Extracurriculars and Milestones. You can also see their progress through graphs and charts to get a snapshot of their progress.
