E's Journal..

... is one stop for parents to keep track for their child's appointments, activities and milestones Thought up by a mum who was tired of making countless notes on scraps of paper and calenders. This platform aims to provide a solution to all planning and organising activities related to your child. You can make notesregarding your child's health, track your their progress at school and mark major milestones. All in one place!

It's as simple as ABC. Sign up! You can add information for all of your children using one account. You can give

access to your partner as well so you both are in the loop. There are different tabs such as Health, School,

Extracurriculars and Milestones. You can also see their progress through graphs and charts to get a snapshot

of their progress.
